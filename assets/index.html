Project Hurricanes Website

The purpose for this project was to determine how much of the energy production and GDP contribution is concentrated in areas of hurricane activity in the U.S.

This research should not be taken as scientific but only as an excercise in web design and data visualization.

We will visualize some of the data we have collected which include modified census data by state and hurricane activity in those areas from the National Weather Services.
